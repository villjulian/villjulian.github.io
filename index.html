Julian Vill

My name is Julian Vill. I am a Postdoc at OvGU Magdeburg working in the algebra group of Thomas Kahle.
Before, I did my Phd at Universität Konstanz with Claus Scheiderer as my advisor.
My research is in real algebraic geometry, convexity and algebraic statistics.
